MUSIC GROOVE


Music Groove is a online platform designed to facilitate music according to users preference. The project leverages Java for its robust backend functionality, ensuring seamless data handling and server-side operations. The frontend, on the other hand, utilizes a combination of HTML, CSS, and the Thyme leaf template provided by Spring Boot, resulting in an engaging and user-friendly interface.
